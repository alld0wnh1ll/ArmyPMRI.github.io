In-Processing Questions

When should I arrive at Purdue?

Plan to arrive two weeks before classes start to complete in-processing and get settled.

What documents do I need for in-processing?

Bring your orders, military ID, medical records, and any PCS documentation.

How do I start the in-processing?

Contact USASD at Fort Jackson through Army Virtual Desktop or NIPR computer access.

Academic Questions

How do I register for classes?

Work with your academic advisor to select courses and register through MyPurdue.

What is the course load requirement?

Maintain full-time status with minimum 8 credit hours per semester.

How do I get in-state tuition?

Submit your orders to the Bursar's office for military classification.

Military Requirements

How do I request leave?

Submit requests through IPPS-A and notify PMRI cadre.

What about PT requirements?

Maintain Army physical fitness standards and complete ACFT as required.

Do I need to complete military training?

Yes, maintain all required training through Army Learning Management System.

Housing & Local Area

What are the housing options?

Answer: Off-campus housing in West Lafayette or Lafayette areas. Contact PMRI cadre for recommendations.


Informative: Many of the previous PMRI participants have purchased homes in the West Lafayette area. Much of the PMRI alumni prefer to rent to PMRI participants. Try to reach out to the current PMRI participants to see if houses or rooms are becoming available. This is a great way to save money. Sharing the rent on these houses allows PMRI students to retain some of their BAH to help with other living costs.

What is the BAH rate?

Check current rates for West Lafayette zip code 47906. Check the following link for current rates: BAH Rate Lookup

Where are the nearest military facilities?

Lafayette Armory and Grissom Air Reserve Base are the closest facilities.

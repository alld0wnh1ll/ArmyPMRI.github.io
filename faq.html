General Questions

When should I arrive at Purdue?

It is recommended to arrive two weeks before classes begin (typically the 3rd week of August). This allows time to:

  • Get settled into housing
  • Complete in-processing
  • Set up local services
  • Familiarize yourself with the area

How do I apply for in-state tuition?

For PMRI Fellowship recipients:

  • Apply for in-state residency once you have orders
  • This is for tuition purposes only (does not affect legal state of residency)
  • Follow directions in your offer letter

For non-Fellowship students:

  • Check Purdue Registrar website for "Residence Classification Information for Military Personnel"

Service-Specific Questions

Air Force In-Processing

Required steps:

  • Complete AFIT CIP Portal registration
  • Set up LeaveWeb account
  • Configure AF Webmail
  • Contact MPF for force management

Navy In-Processing

Required steps:

  • Contact Purdue Navy ROTC admin team
  • Register for health assessment at Great Lakes
  • Complete PHA requirements

Army In-Processing

Required steps:

  • In-process with USASD at Fort Jackson
  • Complete required forms from USASD website (requires Army Virtual Desktop or NIPR computer access)
  • Obtain TRADOC patch

Leave & Passes Information

Army Leave and Pass Policy

As an Army officer participating in PMRI, understanding leave and pass policies is crucial for proper planning and coordination with your academic responsibilities.

Types of Leave

Regular Leave

  • Annual Leave (30 days per year)
  • Must be requested through your chain of command
  • Requires approval from your unit commander
  • Should be coordinated with academic calendar

Special Leave

  • Emergency Leave (Red Cross verified)
  • Convalescent Leave
  • Paternity Leave (21 days)
  • Maternity Leave (12 weeks)

Passes

Types of Passes

  • Regular Pass (3-day weekend)
  • Special Pass (4-day weekend)
  • Block Leave (during academic breaks)

Request Procedures

Required Documentation

  • DA Form 31 (Request and Authority for Leave)
  • Academic calendar conflicts check
  • Unit training schedule verification
  • Emergency contact information

Academic Calendar Considerations

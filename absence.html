Types of Leave

Regular Leave

  • 30 days of leave per year
  • Must be requested through IPPS-A
  • Requires approval from chain of command
  • Plan around academic requirements

Emergency Leave

  • For urgent family matters or emergencies
  • Contact chain of command immediately
  • May require Red Cross verification

Holiday Leave

  • During university breaks
  • Must still be requested and approved
  • Coordinate with academic calendar. The acadmeic calendars can be found here

Passes

Regular Pass

  • Up to 3 days
  • Must remain within 100-mile radius
  • Does not count against leave days

Special Pass

  • Up to 4 days
  • For special circumstances
  • Requires additional approval

Request Procedures

Leave Request Steps

  • Submit request through IPPS-A
  • Notify PMRI Senior Officer
  • Include travel plans if applicable
  • Allow sufficient time for processing

Pass Request Steps

  • Complete pass request form
  • Submit to PMRI Senior Officer
  • Include location and contact information
  • Submit pass request form to Student Detachment Team Leader

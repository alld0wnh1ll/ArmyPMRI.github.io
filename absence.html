Requesting an Absence

Integrated Personnel and Pay System - Army (IPPS-A) is now how the Army handles Absence (Leave/Pass) transactions.

  1. Coordinate with the Army PMRI Senior Officer to ensure your absence will not interfere with academic requirements or graduation timeline.
  2. Download the memo form below and have it signed by the PMRI Senior Officer verifying that your academics will not be negatively impacted.
  3. Log into the Student Detachment Share Portal and identify your current Team Leader and IPPS-A Employee ID. This may change due to PCS or personnel transitions, so check regularly.
  4. Submit your absence request through IPPS-A Absences. Make sure to attach the signed memo to the absence transaction.
  5. Monitor the IPPS-A Portal for updates. Your Team Leader will not email you; they will either approve your request or return it with required changes.
  6. It is your responsibility to monitor the portal and respond to any actions required. If you are unfamiliar with the way IPPS-A works, you can get assistance to one of the more senior Army personnel or reach out to the Army PMRI Senior Officer.

Guidelines

  • If you know you are requesting leave that is outside of the Continental United States (OCONUS), please allow at least 60 days of lead time to meet all Student Detachment requirements for OCONUS travel.
  • Leave is generally predictable in the college environment. It is highly recommended that leave be aligned with academic breaks such as spring break, fall break, winter break, or summer break.
  • While Student Detachment guidelines may change, a good rule of thumb is to provide a minimum of 30 days for CONUS leave requests and 60 days for OCONUS requests for processing by the Student Detachment cadre.
  • If you know you are requesting leave that is outside of the Continental United States (OCONUS), please allow at least 60 days of lead time to meet all Student Detachment requirements for OCONUS travel.
  • All OCONUS (Hawaii is OCONUS) OR Foreign Travel absence requests must include all documents that are required for the destination country on the Electronic Foreign Clearance Guide.
  • If you are planning on taking any academic-related absence that is not Army funded, please refer to the FAQ page for information on non-funded Army academic travel. This transaction requires approval from HRC.

Types of Leave

Regular Leave

  • 30 days of leave per year
  • Must be requested through IPPS-A
  • Requires approval from chain of command
  • Plan around academic requirements

Emergency Leave

  • For urgent family matters or emergencies
  • Contact chain of command immediately
  • May require Red Cross verification

Holiday Leave

  • During university breaks
  • Must still be requested and approved
  • Coordinate with academic calendar. The academic calendars can be found here

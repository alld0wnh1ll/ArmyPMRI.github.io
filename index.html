Hello, I'm Nicholas Harrell

I am a Ph.D. Candidate at Purdue University, specializing in Information Security. My research is centered on unraveling the intricate mechanisms that fuel social media virality. In a world where digital content can shape opinions, drive behaviors, and even influence global events within seconds, understanding what makes certain posts, tweets, or videos go viral is more crucial than ever. My work delves into these dynamics, exploring the factors that turn ordinary content into powerful, widespread phenomena across social media platforms.

Army In-Processing

Required Steps

  • In-process with USASD at Fort Jackson (requires Army Virtual Desktop or NIPR computer access)
  • Complete all required forms through USASD website
  • You can use DFAS SmartVoucher to process your PCS entitlements at this link: https://www.dfas.mil/MilitaryMembers/travelpay/armypcs/Army-PCS-2/
  • Upon authorization of your DFAS SmartVoucher, your new BAH and local entitlements will be updated.
  • Obtain TRADOC patch
  • Set up IPPS-A account for leave management

Academic Information

In-State Tuition/Residency

  • Apply for in-state residency with your orders
  • This is for tuition purposes only
  • Follow directions in your offer letter
  • Check Purdue Registrar website for military personnel classification

Housing Information

Arrival Planning

  • Recommended arrival: Two weeks before classes
  • Check BAH rates for West Lafayette area
  • Contact PMRI cadre for housing recommendations
  • Consider proximity to campus and facilities

Housing and Schools

Arrival Information

Recommended arrival: Two weeks before classes begin (typically 3rd week of August)

This allows time to:

  • Get settled into housing
  • Complete in-processing
  • Set up local services
  • Familiarize yourself with the area

Academic Information

In-State Tuition/Residency

Important for PMRI Fellowship recipients:

  • Apply for in-state residency once you have orders
  • This is for tuition purposes only (does not affect legal state of residency)
  • Follow directions in your offer letter
  • Non-Fellowship students: Check Purdue Registrar website for "Residence Classification Information for Military Personnel"
